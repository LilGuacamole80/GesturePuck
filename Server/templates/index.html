Gesture Control. Redefined.

Control your world.

GesturePuck translates your natural hand movements into powerful actions — so you can present, create, and navigate without ever reaching for a mouse or keyboard.

Where you can control without contact

We built GesturePuck for the people who can't afford to break their flow. Whether you're mid-presentation, deep in a design, or live on stream — your hands are already moving. We just make those movements mean something.

Swipe to advance slides, circle to adjust volume, wave to trigger shortcuts. It all happens in the moment, the way you'd naturally do it anyway.
